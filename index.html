Welcome to Aadhaar Management System

The Aadhaar Management System is a centralized platform to help citizens register, update, and manage their Aadhaar details. Our aim is to make the process simple, secure, and accessible to everyone.

With Aadhaar, you can easily verify your identity for government schemes, financial services, and online transactions. Manage all your Aadhaar services in one place with our secure login system.

Register for Aadhaar

To register for Aadhaar, follow these steps:

  1. Visit your nearest Aadhaar Enrollment Center.
  2. Carry valid documents:
    • Proof of Identity (POI) – Passport, PAN card, Voter ID
    • Proof of Address (POA) – Utility bill, Bank statement
    • Proof of Date of Birth – Birth certificate, School certificate
  3. Provide biometric data – fingerprints, iris scan, and photograph.
  4. Review and confirm all details before submission.
  5. Collect your acknowledgment slip for tracking status.

Once verified, your Aadhaar card will be sent to your address within 90 days.
